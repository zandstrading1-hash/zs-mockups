What we fix

Mobile repair & maintenance, done on-site.

If it can be diagnosed and repaired without a lift, we can usually do it right where your vehicle is parked. Here’s a look at the work we handle most often.

🔌

Diagnostics & Check Engine

Code scans, drivability issues, and pinpoint diagnostics so you know what’s actually wrong before paying for parts.

⚡

Batteries, Starters & Alternators

No-crank, no-start, and charging system failures — tested and replaced on-site so you’re not stranded.

🚧

Brakes

Pads, rotors, calipers, and brake fluid service. Squealing, grinding, or a soft pedal — we’ll inspect and quote before work begins.

🛡

Oil Changes & Fluids

Oil and filter changes, coolant, transmission, and brake fluid services on your schedule, in your driveway.

🔥

Cooling System

Overheating, leaks, thermostats, water pumps, and hose replacement — before a small leak becomes a head gasket.

🔧

Suspension & Steering

Struts, shocks, ball joints, tie rods, sway bar links, and wheel bearings. Clunks, pulls, and rough rides addressed.

⚙

Belts, Hoses & Tune-Ups

Serpentine belts, spark plugs, ignition coils, and routine tune-up work to keep your vehicle running clean.

🛡️

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Looking at a used car or truck? We’ll meet you at the seller and give you an honest mechanic’s opinion before you buy.

About Ace

Local, mobile, and straightforward.

Ace Mobile Mechanic is a Mt Clemens–based mobile auto repair service. The idea is simple: most repairs don’t need a shop bay, a tow truck, or a day off work. We bring the tools, the parts, and the experience to your driveway and get you back on the road.

You’ll talk directly with the mechanic doing the work — no service writer, no upsell script. We’ll tell you what the vehicle needs, what it doesn’t, and what it’ll cost before we start.

  • On-site repairs for cars, trucks, and most light-duty vehicles
  • Honest diagnostics — we won’t sell you parts you don’t need
  • Clear communication by phone or text before, during, and after the job
