What we work on

Services we offer

From a quick oil change to a check-engine-light diagnosis, we keep your vehicle running safely and reliably. Don't see what you need? Give us a call.

Oil changes & routine maintenance

Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes, plus filter replacements and fluid top-offs.

Brakes

Brake pad and rotor replacement, brake inspections, and squeak or pulsation diagnosis.

Check-engine light & diagnostics

Code scans and step-by-step diagnostics so you understand the problem before paying to fix it.

Tires & suspension

Tire rotations, mounting and balancing, plus inspection of shocks, struts, and steering components.

Batteries, starting & charging

Battery testing and replacement, alternator and starter diagnosis, and no-start troubleshooting.

Heating & A/C

Climate-control diagnosis, A/C recharge, and repair of blowers, blend doors, and refrigerant leaks.

Belts, hoses & cooling

Serpentine belts, radiator hoses, water pumps, thermostats, and coolant flushes.

Pre-purchase & used-car inspections

A thorough look before you buy — brakes, fluids, leaks, suspension, and obvious red flags.

About the shop

A local Michigan shop built on doing the work right.

Great Lakes Auto Service LLC is an independent auto repair shop in Macomb County, Michigan. We work on most makes and models — domestic and import — and focus on the everyday maintenance and repairs that keep your daily driver dependable.

Our approach is simple: take the time to diagnose the actual problem, explain what we found in plain English, and quote the work before we start. No surprise add-ons, no pressure.
