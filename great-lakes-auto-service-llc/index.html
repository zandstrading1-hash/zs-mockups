Services

What we work on

From everyday maintenance to the harder problems that pop up after a Michigan winter, our team handles the work most drivers need to keep a car safe and reliable.

Oil & Fluids

Oil Changes & Fluid Service

Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes, plus coolant, transmission, brake, and power-steering fluid checks.

Brakes

Brake Repair

Pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, and full inspections — so you stop short when it matters.

Diagnostics

Check Engine & Diagnostics

Computer diagnostics for warning lights, rough idle, misfires, and drivability issues — with a plain-English explanation of what we found.

Tires

Tires, Rotations & Alignment Checks

Mount and balance, rotations, pressure and tread checks, plus alignment inspections to protect tire life.

Heat & A/C

Heating & Air Conditioning

A/C performance checks, recharges, and heater diagnostics — important in both Michigan summers and winters.

Maintenance

Scheduled Maintenance

Belts, hoses, batteries, filters, wipers, and the routine items that prevent the bigger repairs down the road.
